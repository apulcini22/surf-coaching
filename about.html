About Us

Learn the story behind Surf Right Coaching

Our Story

Founded in 2015, Surf Right has been helping people of all ages and abilities experience the joy and thrill of surfing. What started as a passion project by a lone surf enthusiast has grown into a dedicated team of instructors committed to sharing their love for the ocean and surfing with others.

Meet Your Coach

With over 15 years of surfing experience and 8 years as a certified instructor, I've helped hundreds of students catch their first wave and progress to advanced techniques. My journey with surfing began when I was just 10 years old, and it's been my passion ever since.

I've surfed across the globe, from the legendary breaks of Hawaii to the hidden gems of Indonesia, and I bring this wealth of experience to every lesson. My teaching approach focuses on safety, proper technique, and above all, making sure you have fun while learning this incredible sport.

Our Philosophy

At Surf Right, we believe that surfing is more than just a sportâ€”it's a connection with nature, a form of meditation, and a lifestyle. Our teaching philosophy centers around:

  • Safety First: We prioritize water safety and ocean awareness in every lesson.
  • Individual Approach: Each student learns differently, so we tailor our teaching methods to match your learning style.
  • Progression at Your Pace: Whether you're a cautious beginner or an eager thrill-seeker, we'll help you advance at a comfortable pace.
  • Ocean Respect: We teach not just how to surf, but how to respect and protect our oceans and beaches.
  • Community Building: Surfing brings people together, and we foster a supportive community of surf enthusiasts.
