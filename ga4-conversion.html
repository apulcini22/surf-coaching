GA4 Conversion Testing

Trigger and inspect GA4 dataLayer conversion events for development & QA

Auto-Fired Events (Page Load)

These GA4 conversion events are pushed to window.dataLayer automatically when the page loads or after a timed delay, simulating a real user journey.

page_view Immediate Standard page view with full page context ✓ Fired
view_item Immediate Product view for Surf Coaching Package ✓ Fired
view_item_list +2 seconds User scrolls and sees a product list Pending
purchase +8 seconds Full e-commerce purchase with multi-item cart Pending

Interactive Conversion Events

Click each button to push the corresponding GA4 event to window.dataLayer. Open your browser console or GTM Preview to inspect.
